Temporary Email Addresses

Use qmq.ch when you need a quick, temporary email address. No signup required. It is a great idea if you need to test email workflows like account creation, activity notifications or newsletter sign up.

First time here?

Is this your first time here and you are wondering how this works? Keep reading. qmq.ch gives you disposable inboxes. Without having to sign-up or login. You don't even have to create an inbox before you can start sending emails there. This means that the next time you need a throw away email address you can use some-random-words@qmq.ch and then visit qmq.ch to view the emails sent to that address.
